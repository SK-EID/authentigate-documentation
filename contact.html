Contact

To integrate with the live environment, follow these steps

  1. Request access via our e-Service Portal

  2. Choose a suitable pricing plan based on your usage and business needs.

  3. Provide your live environment details in the order form. See section 2. Client Registration in the Authentigate documentation for required parameters.

  4. Sign the agreement to proceed.

  5. Once your access is configured, we’ll contact you with confirmation and next steps.
